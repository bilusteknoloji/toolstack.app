Quick Date Reminder

Never forget a thing!

Quick Date Reminder helps you keep track of important dates effortlessly. Whether it’s a birthday 🎂, anniversary 🎉, or any special 🥳 event, Quick Date Reminder ensures you never forget 🎗️ a meaningful moment.

Simply add your events, choose whether they should repeat, and let the app handle the rest. With seamless calendar 📆 integration and timely notifications, staying organized has never been easier.

Features

Easy Event Creation

Quickly add birthdays, anniversaries, and special dates.

Recurring Reminders

Set reminders to repeat automatically every year.

Calendar Integration

Sync events directly to your calendar seamlessly and share with loved ones.

Timely Notifications

Get alerts 2 days before, 1 day before, and on the event day.

Simple & Intuitive UI

Designed for ease of use, ensuring quick event management.

Privacy First

Your events are stored only in your selected iCloud or Gmail calendar. Everything stays private and secure on your device.
